How does this actually work?

When someone blocks you, the block applies to your account — not the rest of the internet. So if you open their profile in a logged-out browser, you can still see it. Even a "this account is private" lock screen counts: the profile exists.

That gives us a clean truth table:

→ You can't see them in your app, but they exist publicly = blocked.
→ You can't see them in your app, and they don't exist publicly = the account was deactivated, deleted, or renamed.

This site doesn't scrape, doesn't log in, and stores nothing. Your input never leaves your browser — we just generate the link and ask what you saw.

Not affiliated with Instagram or TikTok. Made with care, not data collection.
